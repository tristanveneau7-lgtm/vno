Mockup of the four-phase VNO app quiz flow — capture info, capture style, capture assets, hit build — designed to fit in sixty seconds of tapping on mobile outside a shop.

Phase 1 · Business ~15 sec

Name, address, phone, hours. I type fast.

Shortcut: paste Google Maps URL → app scrapes the basics → I just verify.

Phase 2 · Style ~15 sec

4 taps: light/dark, type mood, color mood, energy.

My gut says "bold" or "quiet" faster than I say "Montserrat".

Phase 3 · Assets ~20 sec

Upload logo. Snap 1-2 photos of storefront.

Skippable — AI fills gaps if I have nothing.

Phase 4 · Build ~10 sec

One button. Cloud generates everything.

Progress: "Designing… Writing… Deploying…"

Total: ~60 seconds from tap to pitch-ready
